ABOUT US!

Chicken Ni Bai is an inspired food stand to make delicious fried chickens to offer it in the city of Davao. We started our food stand business on May 26,2020 at Sta, Anna Davao Branch to provide crispy and affordable chicken with many more to the students near at our physical store. With that, we become popular in the field of fried chickens industry and made a lot of friends through our customers. We also do deliveries to cater all of our customers who want to taste the food in our menu's.

Chicken Ni Bai was more than just a food stand, it was a gathering place, where strangers became friends over shared plates of delight. Laughter echoed through the streets as people savored every bite, forging memories that would last a lifetime.

We're opened during weekdays and weekends starting from 8:00 AM in the morning till 9:00 PM in the evening, in our store at Narra Branch. What are you waiting for? Be part of our growing family now!
